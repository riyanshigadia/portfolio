Marketing Experiments

Working through a problem, live.

Not a slide deck - the actual sequence of questions I'd ask before recommending anything.

High Website Traffic, Low Conversions

Diagnostic exercise
My First Question

Are people changing their mind, or were they never convinced in the first place?

What I'd Investigate
  • Which page has the highest drop-off?
  • Are visitors coming from the right audience?
  • Is the landing page delivering what the ad promised?
  • Is trust missing (reviews, guarantees, social proof)?
  • Is decision fatigue slowing people down?
  • Compare Bounce Rate & Time on Page across channels to identify low-intent traffic or poor audience targeting.
My Hypothesis

Conversion isn't always about the checkout. It's often about confidence. If someone is unsure, they'll postpone the decision instead of rejecting the product.

My Recommendation
  • Reduce choices.
  • Show reviews earlier.
  • Make shipping and returns visible.
  • Reinforce trust at key decision points.
